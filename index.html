About Us

Baila Toronto is a dance school in Toronto dedicated to teaching Brazilian dance styles with Brazilian methods. We currently provide Samba de Gafieira classes. We also organize events and socials, as well as workshops for many Brazilian dance styles. Check out our schedule and come try a class for free.
